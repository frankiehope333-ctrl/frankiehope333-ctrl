# 🌙 the deep dive · *together*

<!-- 🎨 ART: assets/adventure/together.png — the light warming back up, Marlowe & Cass side by side, the chest below, laughter in bubbles. -->
> *🌇 Strange thing: the deeper they go, the warmer the light gets. Sunset, breaking through.*

With Cass beside her and the creatures lighting the path, the deep stops being
frightening. The lantern-fish picks out the trail; the ray takes the weight she's
been hauling for years. They reach the chest together, laughing in bursts of
bubbles.

And Marlowe notices something she hasn't felt in a long time: she isn't tired
down here. For the first time, she has room to spare.

**What does she do?**
