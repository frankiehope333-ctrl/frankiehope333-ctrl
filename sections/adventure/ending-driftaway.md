> *🌅 The same currents. The same rounds. Nothing lost, and nothing gained.*

She doesn't dive. She doesn't follow the bubbles. She does her rounds, the way
she always has — and the reef stays exactly the same forever.

Comfortable. Small. The glint fades. The bubbles move on without her.

It's a fine life. It's just the only one she'll ever have, down here, doing it
all by hand.
