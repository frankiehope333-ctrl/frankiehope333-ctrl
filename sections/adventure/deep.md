# 🌙 the deep dive · *the descent*

> *🌑 Down where the light gives out. Plum dark. The pressure presses back.*

It's too deep to do alone; the pressure's crushing and the glint is still further
down. Then — help, if she'll take it. A **lantern-fish** drifts up and offers to
light the way. A **ferrying ray** offers to carry her. And there's **Cass** again,
gear humming, perfectly at home in the dark.

**What does Marlowe do?**

- ✨ [**Accept all the help**](together.md) — the light, the ray, the diver
- 💪 [**"I've got it myself"**](alone.md) — she always has
